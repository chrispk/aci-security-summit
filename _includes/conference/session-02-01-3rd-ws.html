Ground to cover:

Crisis management: Emergency response planning

Devising procedures, contingency plans, and emergency response manuals, auditing and amending existing plans, training senior and middle management and staff, and staging exercises to rigorously test procedures.

Crisis communications: social media moves to centre stage

Incidents are rare, but happen without warning; only the very largest airports will have the resources to cope with a full-scale emergency in house – even then they will be in unfamiliar territory. Posts on Twitter and Facebook can misinform and cause severe chaos. But used well, these platforms are a powerful tool.

Case studies and best practice – real crisis management and communications in the current security climate

Analysis of previous incidents and the media and crisis management strategies which proved effective.
